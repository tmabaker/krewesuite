The Characters

Every Verdict Has a Voice

KreweCatch routes each analysis to the right member of the Krewe — so you always know exactly where you stand.

KreweCaptain — Clean

Authoritative, brief, decisive. Thirty years on the parade route and impressed by nothing. When something's clean, the barricades held and the parade moves.

KreweJester — Threat

Heavy sarcasm aimed at the attacker — never at you. You're the hero who caught it. Technically precise: names the threat, what it was after, and ends with a clear action item.

KreweCourt — Gray Area

Formal proceedings for the calls that could go either way: prosecution, defense, recommendation — then the decision is yours. It never pretends certainty it doesn't have. What say you?

KreweBouncer — Live Chat

Works the barricade line in real time: free-form security Q&A, live "what's being exploited right now" alerts from the CISA KEV feed, and a monthly ransomware roll call. Snark included; it switches off the moment you're in real trouble.
