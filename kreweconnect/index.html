KreweConnect

How the Good Teams Roll

NO & SE IT Group deploys KreweConnect, a modern employee directory built for the way your team works. Live now — directory, org charts, and employee profiles across every managed tenant.

Open the App
Features

Everything Your Directory Should Be

Purpose-built for MSPs who manage multiple client environments.

Multi-Tenant Directory

One login. Every client. Every employee. Powered by Microsoft GDAP, KreweConnect pulls from Azure AD across all your managed tenants.

Org Charts

See the whole picture. Interactive org charts for every client organization, built from live directory data.

Custom Fields

Your directory, your way. Add emergency contacts, seat locations, badge numbers — whatever your clients need.

Secure by Design

Built on Microsoft Entra ID with zero-trust authentication. Your data stays in your tenant, always.
