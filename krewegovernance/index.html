Features

Policy Work, Without the Paperwork

In active development — here's what KreweGovernance brings to the Krewe Suite.

Policy Template Library

A curated library of ready-to-tailor policy templates. Each template is written once and reused across every client — no more starting from a blank page.

Guided Variable Wizard

Answer plain-English questions once — company name, key contacts, review cadence — and the wizard fills every policy. Universal answers carry over; only policy-specific ones remain.

Document Assembly

The assembly engine merges each client's answers into the templates and produces finished, client-specific policy documents — consistent, current, and ready to distribute.

Acknowledgment Tracking

Distribute policies to employees and track who has read and acknowledged what — a complete, audit-ready record when compliance questions come knocking.
